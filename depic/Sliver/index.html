Sliver (iOS7+)[Theme]

This theme is based off of CP Digital Darkroom's Sliver tweak. Their tweak shrank your dock blur down to a divider line to divide your apps from your docked apps and had an elegant and minimalistic appearance.

There was a problem though, it was only iOS 8+ compatible, and I wanted to use it on my iPhone 4 (iOS 7). I was inspired by Coolstar's theme for anemone, ClassicDock. Their theme made the dock shaped to the early dock used in iOS6, so I edited the theme to make my version of Sliver that was was compatible with iOS7.

BTW, this Theme only works for Anemone, Not Winterboard so, yeah.

That's it... ENJOY!
